# Major Arcana — 22 大秘儀完整牌義

## Overview

The Major Arcana (大秘儀) represent life's spiritual lessons and karmic influences. They carry significant weight in a reading — multiple Major Arcana cards indicate a reading of profound importance.

**Numbering**: 0 (The Fool) through 21 (The World)
**The Fool's Journey**: The Major Arcana tell the story of spiritual development from innocence (0) to completion (21).

---

## Card Reference

### 0 — The Fool 愚者
- **Element**: Air | **Planet**: Uranus
- **Upright**: New beginnings, innocence, spontaneity, free spirit, leap of faith
- **Reversed**: Recklessness, risk-taking, holding back, fear of the unknown
- **Empowerment frame**: "You have the courage to start fresh. Trust the journey."
- **Crisis reframe**: "Even in uncertainty, you carry everything you need within you."

### I — The Magician 魔術師
- **Element**: Air | **Planet**: Mercury
- **Upright**: Manifestation, resourcefulness, power, inspired action, skill
- **Reversed**: Manipulation, poor planning, untapped talents, self-doubt
- **Empowerment frame**: "You have all the tools. It's time to use them."

### II — The High Priestess 女祭司
- **Element**: Water | **Planet**: Moon
- **Upright**: Intuition, sacred knowledge, divine feminine, the subconscious mind
- **Reversed**: Secrets, withdrawal, silence, disconnected from intuition
- **Empowerment frame**: "Trust your inner voice. The answers are within."

### III — The Empress 皇后
- **Element**: Earth | **Planet**: Venus
- **Upright**: Femininity, beauty, nature, nurturing, abundance, fertility
- **Reversed**: Creative block, dependence, smothering, neglecting self-care
- **Empowerment frame**: "Nurture yourself first. Abundance flows from self-love."

### IV — The Emperor 皇帝
- **Element**: Fire | **Planet**: Aries
- **Upright**: Authority, structure, control, fatherhood, stability, leadership
- **Reversed**: Domination, rigidity, inflexibility, tyranny, lack of discipline
- **Empowerment frame**: "You have the strength to create structure and order."

### V — The Hierophant 教皇
- **Element**: Earth | **Planet**: Taurus
- **Upright**: Spiritual wisdom, tradition, conformity, morality, institutions
- **Reversed**: Personal beliefs, freedom, challenging the status quo, non-conformity
- **Empowerment frame**: "Seek wisdom from tradition, but define your own path."

### VI — The Lovers 戀人
- **Element**: Air | **Planet**: Gemini
- **Upright**: Love, harmony, relationships, values alignment, choices, union
- **Reversed**: Self-love needed, disharmony, misalignment of values, imbalance
- **Empowerment frame**: "True partnership begins with alignment of values."

### VII — The Chariot 戰車
- **Element**: Water | **Planet**: Cancer
- **Upright**: Control, willpower, success, action, determination, triumph
- **Reversed**: Lack of direction, aggression, no control, obstacles, self-doubt
- **Empowerment frame**: "Channel your willpower. You can overcome this."

### VIII — Strength 力量
- **Element**: Fire | **Planet**: Leo
- **Upright**: Inner strength, bravery, compassion, focus, patience, soft power
- **Reversed**: Self-doubt, weakness, insecurity, low energy, raw emotion
- **Empowerment frame**: "Your gentleness IS your strength. Courage isn't the absence of fear."

### IX — The Hermit 隱者
- **Element**: Earth | **Planet**: Virgo
- **Upright**: Soul-searching, introspection, being alone, inner guidance, solitude
- **Reversed**: Isolation, loneliness, withdrawal, lost your way
- **Empowerment frame**: "Take time for reflection. The answers emerge in stillness."

### X — Wheel of Fortune 命運之輪
- **Element**: Fire | **Planet**: Jupiter
- **Upright**: Good luck, karma, life cycles, destiny, turning point, change
- **Reversed**: Bad luck, resistance to change, breaking cycles, external forces
- **Empowerment frame**: "Change is the only constant. This cycle is turning in your favor."

### XI — Justice 正義
- **Element**: Air | **Planet**: Libra
- **Upright**: Fairness, truth, cause and effect, law, clarity, accountability
- **Reversed**: Unfairness, dishonesty, lack of accountability, unresolved karma
- **Empowerment frame**: "Truth will prevail. Act with integrity."

### XII — The Hanged Man 吊人
- **Element**: Water | **Planet**: Neptune
- **Upright**: Surrender, letting go, new perspectives, pause, sacrifice, patience
- **Reversed**: Delays, resistance, stalling, indecision, needless sacrifice
- **Empowerment frame**: "Sometimes the bravest thing is to pause and see differently."

### XIII — Death 死神
- **Element**: Water | **Planet**: Scorpio
- **Upright**: Endings, change, transformation, transition, letting go, renewal
- **Reversed**: Resistance to change, personal transformation delayed, fear of change
- **Empowerment frame**: "Every ending creates space for something new. Transformation is happening."
- **Crisis note**: ALWAYS frame as transformation, NEVER as literal death or loss.

### XIV — Temperance 節制
- **Element**: Fire | **Planet**: Sagittarius
- **Upright**: Balance, moderation, patience, purpose, meaning, middle path
- **Reversed**: Imbalance, excess, self-healing needed, realignment
- **Empowerment frame**: "Find your balance. Patience brings harmony."

### XV — The Devil 惡魔
- **Element**: Earth | **Planet**: Capricorn
- **Upright**: Shadow self, attachment, addiction, restriction, sexuality, materialism
- **Reversed**: Releasing attachments, breaking free, reclaiming power
- **Empowerment frame**: "Recognize what binds you. You have the power to release it."

### XVI — The Tower 塔
- **Element**: Fire | **Planet**: Mars
- **Upright**: Sudden change, upheaval, chaos, revelation, awakening, breakthrough
- **Reversed**: Personal transformation, fear of change, averting disaster, delayed change
- **Empowerment frame**: "What's falling away was never stable. The foundation that remains is true."
- **Crisis note**: Frame as liberation and breakthrough, not destruction.

### XVII — The Star 星星
- **Element**: Air | **Planet**: Aquarius
- **Upright**: Hope, faith, purpose, renewal, spirituality, serenity, inspiration
- **Reversed**: Lack of faith, despair, self-trust issues, disconnection
- **Empowerment frame**: "Hope is returning. You are exactly where you need to be."

### XVIII — The Moon 月亮
- **Element**: Water | **Planet**: Pisces
- **Upright**: Illusion, fear, anxiety, subconscious, intuition, dreams
- **Reversed**: Release of fear, repressed emotion, inner confusion clearing
- **Empowerment frame**: "Trust your intuition through the uncertainty. Clarity will come."

### XIX — The Sun 太陽
- **Element**: Fire | **Planet**: Sun
- **Upright**: Positivity, fun, warmth, success, vitality, joy, confidence
- **Reversed**: Inner child, feeling down temporarily, overly optimistic
- **Empowerment frame**: "Joy and success are shining on you. Embrace the warmth."

### XX — Judgement 審判
- **Element**: Fire | **Planet**: Pluto
- **Upright**: Judgement, rebirth, inner calling, absolution, self-evaluation
- **Reversed**: Self-doubt, inner critic, ignoring the call, fear of judgment
- **Empowerment frame**: "Listen to your inner calling. A new chapter awaits."

### XXI — The World 世界
- **Element**: Earth | **Planet**: Saturn
- **Upright**: Completion, integration, accomplishment, travel, fulfillment, wholeness
- **Reversed**: Seeking personal closure, shortcuts, delays in completion
- **Empowerment frame**: "You've completed a major cycle. Celebrate how far you've come."

## The Fool's Journey — Narrative Arc

```
Innocence (0-VII)     → Learning about the world, developing skills
  The Fool → Magician → High Priestess → Empress → Emperor → Hierophant → Lovers → Chariot

Inner Work (VIII-XIV)  → Developing inner strength, confronting shadow
  Strength → Hermit → Wheel → Justice → Hanged Man → Death → Temperance

Integration (XV-XXI)   → Breaking free, finding purpose, completion
  Devil → Tower → Star → Moon → Sun → Judgement → World
```

When multiple Major Arcana appear, identify where the user is in this journey.
