# Minor Arcana — 56 張小秘儀按花色分類

## Overview

The Minor Arcana represent daily life events and experiences. They show how the Major Arcana's big themes play out practically.

---

## Wands 權杖 (Fire Element 🔥)

**Theme**: Action, ambition, creativity, willpower, passion, energy
**Life area**: Career, projects, creative pursuits, motivation

| Card | Upright | Reversed |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Ace** | Inspiration, new opportunities, growth, potential | Delays, lack of direction, missed opportunity |
| **Two** | Future planning, progress, decisions, discovery | Fear of unknown, lack of planning, personal goals |
| **Three** | Progress, expansion, foresight, overseas opportunities | Obstacles in plans, delays in travel, frustration |
| **Four** | Celebration, harmony, homecoming, community | Transition, personal celebration, inner harmony |
| **Five** | Disagreement, competition, tension, diversity of thought | Inner conflict, avoiding conflict, finding resolution |
| **Six** | Success, public recognition, progress, self-confidence | Egotism, fall from grace, lack of recognition |
| **Seven** | Challenge, competition, perseverance, standing your ground | Feeling overwhelmed, giving up, defensive |
| **Eight** | Movement, fast pace, quick decisions, rapid action | Delays, frustration, internal alignment needed |
| **Nine** | Resilience, courage, persistence, last stand, boundaries | Paranoia, defensive, overwhelm, giving up |
| **Ten** | Burden, responsibility, hard work, stress, achievement | Doing too much, delegate, release burdens |
| **Page** | Exploration, excitement, freedom, adventure, new ideas | Setbacks, lack of direction, procrastination |
| **Knight** | Energy, passion, adventure, action, impulsiveness | Haste, scattered energy, delays in plans |
| **Queen** | Courage, confidence, determination, joy, vibrancy | Self-respect needed, jealousy, temperamental |
| **King** | Natural leader, vision, entrepreneur, honor, big picture | Impulsive, overbearing, unrealistic expectations |

## Swords 寶劍 (Air Element 🌬️)

**Theme**: Thoughts, words, conflict, truth, intellect, decisions
**Life area**: Communication, mental challenges, conflict resolution, truth-seeking

| Card | Upright | Reversed |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Ace** | Breakthrough, clarity, sharp mind, truth, new ideas | Confusion, chaos, lack of clarity, brutality |
| **Two** | Difficult decisions, weighing options, stalemate, avoidance | Indecision, confusion, information overload |
| **Three** | Heartbreak, emotional pain, sorrow, grief, separation | Recovery, forgiveness, moving on from pain |
| **Four** | Rest, relaxation, meditation, contemplation, recuperation | Exhaustion, burnout, stagnation, restlessness |
| **Five** | Conflict, disagreements, competition, defeat, winning at cost | Reconciliation, making amends, past resentment |
| **Six** | Transition, change, rite of passage, releasing baggage | Resistance to change, unfinished business, delays |
| **Seven** | Betrayal, deception, getting away with something, strategy | Imposter syndrome, self-deception, coming clean |
| **Eight** | Restriction, imprisonment, powerlessness, self-limiting beliefs | Self-acceptance, new perspective, freedom |
| **Nine** | Anxiety, worry, fear, depression, nightmares, overwhelm | Inner turmoil releasing, hope, reaching out |
| **Ten** | Painful endings, deep wounds, betrayal, loss, crisis | Recovery, regeneration, resisting the inevitable |
| **Page** | Curiosity, restlessness, mental energy, new ideas, vigilant | Deception, manipulation, all talk no action |
| **Knight** | Ambitious, action-oriented, fast-thinking, driven | No direction, disregard for consequences, restless |
| **Queen** | Independent, unbiased judgment, clear boundaries, direct | Overly emotional, easily influenced, cold-hearted |
| **King** | Mental clarity, intellectual power, authority, truth | Manipulative, tyrannical, abuse of power, cold |

**Crisis note**: Three of Swords, Nine of Swords, and Ten of Swords require extra sensitivity. Always reframe as passing states with healing ahead.

**Theme**: Finances, career, health, material world, physical security
**Life area**: Money, work, property, physical health, practical matters

| Card | Upright | Reversed |
|------|---------|----------|

| **Five** | Financial loss, poverty, lack mindset, isolation, worry | Recovery, spiritual poverty, regaining stability |
| **Six** | Giving, receiving, sharing wealth, generosity, charity | Self-care needed, strings attached, one-sided generosity |
| **Seven** | Long-term view, sustainable results, perseverance, patience | Lack of long-term vision, limited success, impatience |
| **Eight** | Apprenticeship, skill development, repetitive work, mastery | Self-development, perfectionism, no ambition |
| **Nine** | Abundance, luxury, self-sufficiency, financial independence | Self-worth issues, superficiality, living beyond means |
| **Ten** | Wealth, financial security, family, long-term success, inheritance | Financial failure or trouble, loneliness, loss |
| **Page** | Ambition, desire, diligence, good news, new beginnings | Lack of progress, procrastination, learning from failure |
| **Knight** | Efficiency, routine, conservatism, methodical, hardworking | Boredom, laziness, feeling stuck, perfectionism |
| **Queen** | Nurturing, practical, providing, homebody, financial security | Self-care needed, work-life balance, smothering |
| **King** | Wealth, business, leadership, security, discipline, abundance | Financially inept, obsessed with wealth, stubborn |

## Court Card Personality Mapping

Court cards often represent people in the querent's life or aspects of themselves:

| Court | Age/Energy | Role | When representing self |
|-------|-----------|------|----------------------|
| **Page** | Young/Fresh | Student, messenger, child | Learning phase, new perspective |
| **Knight** | Active/Dynamic | Adventurer, pursuer | Taking action, pursuing goals |
| **Queen** | Mature/Receptive | Nurturer, intuitive guide | Inner wisdom, receptive power |
| **King** | Mature/Active | Authority, leader, provider | Mastery, external authority |
